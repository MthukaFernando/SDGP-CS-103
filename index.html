Our features

CookiMate is a smart social cooking assistant that combines AI guidance, voice interaction, and a friendly mascot to make cooking easier and more enjoyable for beginners. From step-by-step help in the kitchen to a supportive community and health-conscious tools, CookiMate brings everything you need into one simple experience.

Interactive AI Mascot

Your smart cooking buddy that guides, helps, and motivates you.

Unit Converter

No math, no mess — get the right measurement every time.

In-Built Recipies

Easy recipes that actually fit your time, skills, and ingredients.

Community

Cook together, share ideas, and get inspired by real people.

Meal Planner

Turn “What should I cook?” into a ready-to-go plan in seconds.

Earn Points

Cook smarter, waste less, and get rewarded for every meal.
